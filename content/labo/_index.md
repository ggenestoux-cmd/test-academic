DISP-lab (Decision & Information Systems for Production systems, UR4570), gathers researchers and teacher-researchers from Université de Lyon around a double expertise in **Industrial Engineering** and **Information technology for business**.

To meet the scientific challenges imposed by the ongoing changes within the socio-economic world, our research deals with t**he design and deployment of decision-making methods and information systems in order to improve the performance, the agility and the resilience of goods and services production systems and comprehensive supply chains**.

Its double expertise, based on competencies in modelling, operational research, simulation, software engineering, artificial intelligence, planning, scheduling, and decision-making, allows studying these complex systems by addressing simultaneously their technical, structural, organizational and human-related dimensions.

Its members belong to 4 institutions of Université de Lyon : INSA Lyon, Université Lumière Lyon 2 and Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1, as supervising institutions, as well as Université Jean Monnet de Saint Etienne as partner institution.

Their activities consist in:

* The development of new knowledge, its transfer and application in companies, as part of scientific theoretical and applied research,
* The dissemination and exploitation of these results,
* The training for and through research, particularly by doctoral thesis supervision,
* The scientific expertise and support for research,
* And the dissemination of culture and scientific information.
